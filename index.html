It was a dark and stormy night...

The night was Halloween of 1984 in Hawkins, Indiana.

A boy was walking alone down a long and winding path.

Suddenly a creature appears in the fog on a bicycle!

It has tentacles and is generally terrifying.

There is slime oozing from its fins and blood covering its tentacles.

But enough about the bicycle, you might be saying. Does the creature look friendly?
